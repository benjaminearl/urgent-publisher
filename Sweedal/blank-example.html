The Climate Decoder

This project seeks to archive the animal behaviours of extreme weather patterns of the African Asian Subcontinents, and understand that these observations have proved to be talismans for their people for thousands of years. The climate change in the Northern Hemisphere of the globe shifts this prompt to question what signs might testify to be useful and encourage us to become effective readers of the natural signs of change.
