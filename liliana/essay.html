Architectural Obsolescence: Progress, beauty and nostalgia in the urban environment

In the paper we will consider the concept of architectural obsolescence with specific regard to how concepts of Beauty underpin urban development. By considering the nature of progress through an anthropological lens we will further explore how the drive to renew creates a sense of memory and nostalgia.
